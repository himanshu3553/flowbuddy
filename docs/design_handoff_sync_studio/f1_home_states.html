How Sync works
From one recording to live, trustworthy answers — in five steps.
×
videocam
STEP 1
Record your product
Click through your product while talking out loud. The Sync browser extension captures the screen, your voice and every click.
menu_book
STEP 2
Sync builds your knowledge base
It automatically turns that recording into clean, step-by-step workflows. No manual writing required.
task_alt
STEP 3
Approve what the copilot can use
Review the workflows and approve them with one click. The copilot only ever answers from what you approve.
code
STEP 4
Add the copilot to your app
Copy one line of code and paste it into your product.
forum
STEP 5
Your customers get instant answers
The copilot answers your customer's questions right inside your app with sources and an honest “I don’t know yet” when it’s unsure.
autorenew
It gets better on its own. Every question the copilot can’t answer becomes a “record this next” tip so your help section keeps improving.

How to record
Capture a workflow with the Sync Recorder — about 15 minutes.
×
extension
STEP 1
Install & connect the recorder
Add the Sync Recorder to Chrome, then click “Connect with Sync” to link it to your workspace.
fiber_manual_record
STEP 2
Open your product and press Start
Go to your live product, open the extension, and hit Start recording.
mic
STEP 3
Click through a workflow, narrating
Do the task for real while talking out loud — what you’re doing and why. Sync captures the screen, your voice, clicks and pages.
flag
STEP 4
Mark each new workflow
Starting a different task? Hit “Mark new workflow” so Sync keeps them as separate, clean guides.
cloud_upload
STEP 5
Stop, and it uploads itself
Press “Stop & upload.” Your session uploads securely and Sync turns it into your Knowledge Base.
tips_and_updates
Narrate as you go. Saying what you’re doing and why is what makes the copilot’s answers accurate — and it’s masked for PII before upload.
