F2 · THE LOOP
“Record this next” is the differentiator — the copilot’s own honest declines tell Fiona exactly what to record. The KB compounds with use.
F3–F4 · CAPTURE
Recordings come from the Chrome extension — multi-layer (screen · voice · DOM · events · routes). PII is masked in-browser before upload; uploads survive nav + retry.
F5 · TRUST GATE
Approval = one click on a workflow, not authoring an article. The copilot answers only from approved workflows — never raw or un-approved KB.
F6 · KB RICHNESS
Selector + route + expected-outcome (not lossy prose) is what makes answers context-aware now — and actionable “show me” later. Citations point to the workflow/step.
F7 · ACTIVATION
North-star metric: time-to-first-embedded-copilot. One <script> + public key + origin allowlist. Rotatable key; per-workspace rate limit.
F8 · ROI STORY
Deflection (resolved without handoff) is what you sell. Honest declines are a feature, not a failure — they feed straight back into the record-next loop.
NOT IN PHASE 1
Article editor & public Help Portal (the by-products) are frozen — their Studio UI was removed for the copilot-only release. Self-validation / freshness is Phase 3. These would slot in as new nav items later.
